About Us

Zoomir is yet another zoo, located in New York City. Yes, surprisingly, there are a lot of different zoos in New York City, most of which are supported by the Wildlife Conservation Society, which is a really great organization that supports a lot of Zoos around the world. But What makes Zoomir so special? Probably the fact that it is just a random website project, written on really basic HTML, CSS, and a bit of Bootstrap, that was made by Zamir Karimov, a student at the Urban Assembly Gateway School for Technology. So technically, this whole Zoomir, all information and everything that belongs to it, does not exist in real life, if it was not obvious before, considering that the location of the Zoo is set as Hudson River, NY, and working hours are random. But let's pretend it is a serious website with thousands of visitors every hour. They want to visit this zoo more than visiting their own pets after work. If there were an option to pretend so, what would the content of this page look like? Probably some kind of good pictures on the sides of the page, really interesting and engaging text about the long history of the beautiful zoo, probably information about sponsors, because let's be realistic, every zoo need some kind of outside funding to continue its work, because most of the times, ticket prices are not covering even full cost of food that required for all animals that are being held there. So zoos need to have and show partners and sponsors, so other organizations also think about donating some money for such a "great" thing, like the zoo. But talking about “great” things, are Zoos actually great? Actually, there are many people who don't like Zoos because of the way they are treating their animals and how animals are literally jailed for their whole life, without ever visiting the actual wildlife. Some people might argue that these animals wouldn't survive in the wild, which might be true, but the whole idea of capturing animals for their whole life just to showcase them to some people who didn't find better family entertainment than watching animals walking in their cells goallessly feels a bit inhuman and awkward. But let's pretend it is a normal thing, which technically is, but let's pretend that Zoomir is the best place for you and your family, and we are always welcome for you!
