General run-down on what this website is about.

This website will be talking about 2 species of birds of prey, that being the vultures and owls. While there are multiple other birds of prey I fear I am not willing to do more than what is assigned. Each page will be talking about 3 species of either owls or vultures depending on the one chosen. They will list random information about these birds (hopefully interesting enough to engage someone for long enough). Here is a crow as an example of what is to come.

  • Linda Tanner, CC BY 2.0, via Wikimedia Commons

The crow, coming from the genus "Corvus", is considered a songbird belonging to the larger family "Corvidae". While usually being mixed with the raven, (also belonging to the genus Corvus) they are considered smaller than ravens.
