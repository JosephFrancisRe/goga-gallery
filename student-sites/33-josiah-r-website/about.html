About Re's Pizza

Re's Pizza is named after the educator, Mr. Joseph Francis Re. We have been serving Italian food since 500 CE. What began as a small family recipe has grown into one of New York City's most ironically named pizzerias.

What makes Re's Pizza "ironically named," is the roots that shape every pie wie make. We focus on quality ingredients, classic techniques, and zero pineapple. That commitment has made us a favorite across our six NYC locations.
