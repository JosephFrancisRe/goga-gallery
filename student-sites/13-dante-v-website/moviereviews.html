    The Dark Knight

    The Dark Knight

  • Story:9.1/10
  • Characters:9.0/10
  • Performances:9.4/10
  • Music:8.6/10
  • Final Rating:9.2/10

    • My Review

  • The Reasoning:First of all, what a beautiful movie. The Christoper Nolan directed movie was a hit with the viewers and the box office. The story was one of the best bringing the arguably greatest villain in all of fiction into live action. The characters that were in this were appling to any viewer who loves action packed movies. Although there was criticism for the casting of Heath Ledger, he had a extraordinary performance, maybe one of the best I've ever seen. Although the music was not up to par with the rest of the movie, it still gives the film a pleasuring experience. If you love superheros, this will be one of the greatest movies you will ever watch.

    • Overall:9.2/10

    The Five Nights at Freddys Movie

    Five Nights at Freddys Movie

  • Story:7.5/10
  • Characters:8.2/10
  • Performances:8.5/10
  • Music:8.5/10
  • Final Rating:8.0/10

    • My Review

  • The Reasoning:The story was not great,it lacked from the original fan-adored game made 2014 by Scott Cawthon. The characters were also lacking a little bit of the blood-curdling horror the previous frightning games been able to achieve. Although this movie was able to have a total of 297 million in the box office, it still seemed dull to many viewers only receiving a 33% as of October 22nd 2025 for critics. Fans did however enjoy this movie, enough for it to have a 86% for the fan reviews. Overall this movie brought tons of enjoyment for the fans but steered off from the original games to much for it to be distinguished as a Five Nights at Freddys movie.

    • Overall:8.0/10
