Our Story


We started off as two highschool juniors with a crazy dream. After we heard that our school in Hell's Kitchen was closing we decided to buy the building. We were very broke so it took awhile to raise the funds, but once we had everything we demolished the entire building for our dream. For five years we worked tirelessly to mimic the natural habitat for a variety of animals across the world. After a while we expanded our perimeters to meet the needs of our beloved creatures. From the beginning we have given free admission to all living in the city because we believe that new yorkers deserve to see a natural "jungle" in the "concrete jungle". In 2029 we created an artifical island on the Hudson River that connected New York to New Jersey, this allowed for the creation for our famous aquatic exhibitions. We thank the city and our donors for making free zoo in the heart of New York City possible! We aim to expose and educate all in the five boroughs about the wild life that is sadly under threat all over the world!
