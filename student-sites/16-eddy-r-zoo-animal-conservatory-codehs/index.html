Who you'll meet

Free admission to all New Yorkers!

South American Capybara

These passive creatures are friendly to just about any animal. They are semi-aqautic rodents found in South America.

Bengal Tiger

It's bright orange and stripes are excellent at concealing itself around prey. The Bengal Tiger is relentless.

Grey Wolf

Travels in packs. Native to North America and Europe, decline in population due to poaching.

Artic Seal

The artic seal is a semi-aquatic animal. Their natural habitat is ice-cold waters.

Peacock

We let these very beautiful creatures roam free around our zoo. The peacock uses it's pattern to attract mates.
