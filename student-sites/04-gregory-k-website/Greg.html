Tis' the review

The movie we will be reviewing today is...

Meh...

Let me preface by saying that this not a "bad" movie. It is, however, the worst in the series. First off, this was the only "28" movie not directed by Danny Boyle! This is a spanish production! The set is good, but they also use "In The House In A Heartbeat" way too much. This overuse takes away the impact from a very good song. The story is too incoherrent as well. Many bystanders run towards the infected for no reason at all! Soldiers don't start opening fire on everyone immediately (I thought their top priority was making sure the Rage Virus was gone from Britain)! Even the "main zombie", Don, somehow tracks down our main characters time and time again without fail! Overall...6/10.
