History

The historic website "Greg Gets It!", as grandiose as it is, started from very humble beginnings. It all started in 1952, Midtown NY, when Greg got fed up with his circumstances. Every day he would scroll and scroll to find a good movie review blog, but to no avail. That was when he vowed to create his own. Learning every little bit there was to know about html and CSS, he coded and made the "Best Movie Review Website on the Internet". Seconds after it's creation, thousands of people lined up outside of his apartment, eager to work for no pay as long as they could be a part of this glorious achievement. Quickly everyone knew the "Greg gets It!" name, an office was built and everyone in the company soared to wonderous success!

Goals

The "Greg Gets It!" IP was founded on a very specific set of core values and goals; These being Excellence, Perfection, and Total Domination. Building off of these core values, the "Greg Gets It!" team quickly became the most talented workers on the planet! The most obvious example would be it's founder, Greg. He is the genius behind "Greg Gets It!".
