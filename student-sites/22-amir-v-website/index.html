Green Valley Zoo Nestled in the heart of nature, Green Valley Zoo is a vibrant sanctuary where wildlife, conservation, and education come together. Spanning acres of lush greenery, the zoo offers a safe and enriching home to animals from around the world — from majestic lions and playful primates to colorful tropical birds and gentle herbivores. At Green Valley Zoo, we are committed to providing not just an unforgettable experience for our visitors, but also the highest standards of care for our animals. Our spacious, naturalistic enclosures are designed to mimic the animals' natural habitats, ensuring their well-being and encouraging natural behaviors.
