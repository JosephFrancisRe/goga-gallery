In 1996, ATLUS released Persona for the first time and it was a major hit, this led to the creation of 5 more sequels which are all spectacular games.

Our Mission

Our mission is to give good pizza that lets people know about the Persona franchise and how good the games are.

What is Persona?

The Persona franchise consists of turn-based RPG games that all have an incredible story, the stories take place in high school with each protagonist being a high schooler and living their normal life until mysterious things happen, each different with each game. Each game has their own theme that they cover as well, Persona 3's theme focuses on death and finding the meaning in life, Persona 4's theme focuses on truth and acceptance, and Persona 5's theme is about freedom and rebellion against societal injustice.
