Pizza and Side Dishes

Pizzas

Pararoach Pizza

This pizza is our first ever dish! Served with live cockroaches leeches, and worms, this pizza has acted as the very base of our company. The insides of your body may feel a little weird and itchy while and after eating this dish, but there's nothing to worry about, that's just the great aftertaste! If it makes you feel uncomfortable, we suggest to order our Sweet Green Tea to wash it down. The Crimson Lemonade is also a good pick with the leeches.


Moldy Pizza

While looking for toppings in the sewers, we accidentally dropped a to be Pararoach Pizza in the murky waters. It took us a while to find it, but once we recovered the slice we saw that it had gained a tasty green topping that we have never thought about. It was so delicious that we have decided to make it a new addition to our menu and create more of them!


Gnasher Pizza

With the helpful sacrifices and resources that are our customers and the complex rituals commited by our staff, we were able to create a new creature: the Gnasher Pizza! This pizza is alive and hungers for human flesh. You and your meal will fight a glorious battle in a whirlwind of sauce, cheese, and teeth!


Pineapple Pizza

This stuff is disgusting! Out of all the items on here, this is probably our least favorite. We still don't know why we put it on our list. What kind of monster puts fruit on this?! Some food combinations just simply shouldn't be mixed, I'll tell you that...



Side Orders


Jar of Centipedes

A great side dish with more bugs! Also commonly paired with the pararoach pizza, this delicate glass is full of live centipedes that crawl and move in interesting patterns! Along with the Sweet Green Tea, you will have the perfect set meal for 75% off!


Special Salad

For those who want a more vegetarian side course, we have the Special Salad! Grown and harvested directly from the ground, these fresh greens are healthily grown from our gardens. We made sure not to clean them too, so as to preserve the salad's natural taste.


Otherworld Soup

With magic powered by gathered resources and generous...tips, we've constructed a complex rift through spacetime that allowed us to entan entirely different reality. Within this strange dimension, we hunted strange, grotesque creatures and made this soup as a side dish! As for what it's made of...we have no idea, but it tastes great!
