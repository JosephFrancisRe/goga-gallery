Drinks

Sweet Green Tea

Made with water, honey, camellia sinensis leaves, and paraquat, this delicious toxin is one of our new addictions to our restaurant. It's usually paired with our Pararoach pizza, where you can either drink it while eating or dip the pizza it into the liquid like a biscuit. It's so good that you'll feel like you'll sleep forever after drinking it!


Brain Smoothie

While examining our different topics and flavors, we noticed a peculiar substance dripping out from our livestock. We later learned that this thing was called a "brain." It's great and tastes a lot like strawberry! Our green friends who visit our establishments seem to really like this kind of smoothie.


Crimson Lemonade

We noticed how most pizza shops offer lemonade as a drink to go with their foods. We had most of the ingredients, along with some cherries to sweeten the flavor, but we ran out of leftover water to boil. We found a great replacement, though many of the customers who order the drink share common traits of being weirdly pale and slurping the lemonade with a pair of sharp fangs.


Sparkling Water

Why do people like their sparkling water cold? No, actually, how do they even make it that cold? Sparkling water is perfect the way it is, put in a pot and heated on the stove! You'll be served it straight from the kitchen and in the pot. Make sure to drink it quickly though, eventually it'll turn into normal, boring water.
