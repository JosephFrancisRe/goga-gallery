About

History of Wacky Pizza

Our establishment began in 996 CE, right around the time the very creation of pizza existed! Originating in Italy, we were a group of cooks who saw the potential these foods had. An endless amount of posible toppings, and yet nobody has ever even attempted these beautiful ideas.
So, we decided to try, creating our first ever recipe: the Pararoach pizza! We handed it over to a taste-tester, who's face scrunched up in delight as he fell over and began spasming on the ground with yells of joy. He told us that it was painful and absolutely appaling, which meant it had great spicing, and that they would make sure to have people come over to our restaurant. In short, this unorthodox combination was a huge success! Since we got approved we set up our serving times to be in dark alleyways at night, out in the middle of nowhere, or in the caverns of cities. Why, you may ask? Well, while we do get more people coming over when on the surface, some excited customers kept getting skittery and chaotic when we served our foods in the daylight. It was great at first, but then would invite weird people into our restaurants after recieving their order, who caused some trouble for our staff. So we decided to locate our restaurants to more remote, safe areas where our staff could work in peace, and we haven't changed our ways since!


Hiring

We are looking for eager, lively workers who will bring joy and excitement to our establishment. Applicants with high education and degrees in prisons, mental asylums, crime, and the depths of hell are well appreciated and more likely to join our restaurants. Creativity is also highly valued! If you can help us expand our business and create new pizza toppings and creations, then you are a friend in our eyes!
Once you have joined our staff, you will have your mortal coil shredded away from your being. Immortal and energetic, we will serve our pizza to others till the end of time itself!
