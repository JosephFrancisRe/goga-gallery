Meet Our Amazing Cast!

Meet the wonderful cast of characters that truly make your experience at Walter Wotlamb's Pizza truly one of a kind!

Walter Wotlamb

Meet the main man himself! Walter is a rat that loves making children smile!

Platy

Platy is a platypus that plays the violin! She usually plays all day and night, helping anyone in a bad mood.

Pete

Pete is a pig that absolutely loves anything pizza, which is where his love for baking came from. He's never seen far from Chris the Cake.

Ferr

Ferr, being a cowboy, loves adventure and danger! They spend all their time rounding up bandits in Cowboy's Saloon!
