About Whoopsies! Gelato

Our Founders and Our beginnings

Our founders, Wyatt "Whoopsie" Jiménez and Marshall Moore have been the proud owners of Whoopsies! Gelato stores since 2022. They devote much time and effort into the franchise, their goal to bring nothing but smiles never faltering.

Whoopsies! Gelato began as a mere concept in winter of 2015 when Wyatt had gotten into an accident. While he was biking down a slope towards his grandma's house, he slipped out of control due to the icy pavement. He had broken his arm, which led to him not getting out of the house often. Marshall made sure to visit, always bringing gelato as it was Wyatt's favorite treat. Somewhere between jokes about falling and ice cream, an idea came to their heads.

Whoopsies! Gelato wasn't always the name, as there were a few different names that were experimented with. Originally, the final name was going to be Broken Cone Ice Cream. However, Wyatt and Marshall found it to sound way too serious, and a little on the nose about Wyatt's fall. They instead went for a more playful route, calling it Whoopsies Gelato last minute. The exclaimation mark came as a later addition, since the two often found themselves exclaiming the "whoopsies" before saying gelato. Unfortunately, Wyatt and Marhall did not know the difference between ice cream and gelato, and it was too late to rebrand.

In May of 2022, they had finally gotten their first store in Brooklyn up and running. It was small, but it was the first step towards something great. Today, there are three Whoopsies! Gelato stores across New York; two in Brooklyn and one in Manhattan. The success seen so far is more than Wyatt or Marshall could have imagined, and it seems to be only up from here.

What is to come?

By 2027, we plan to open up two more stores. One in Queens, one in the Bronx. The overall goal is to reach all across New York, and maybe even further than that!

Everything Whoopsies! Gelato has today is thanks to its wonderful customers and community. We hope you'll join us, too!
