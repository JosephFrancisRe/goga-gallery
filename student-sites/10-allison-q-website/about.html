About Us

Mario's Pizzeria

Welcome to Mario's Pizzeria, where every slice satisfies your taste buds! Our journey began in a small kitchen in Brooklyn, New York City, over 40 years ago. Mario, a passionate cook with a love for fresh ingredients and traditional recipes, started making pizza.

Fast forward to today, Mario's Pizzeria has brought that authentic spirit across the ocean to your neighborhood. We’ve stayed true to our roots by baking every pizza in a wood-fired oven, ensuring a crispy crust with a smoky aroma that will transport you straight to the streets of New York City.

At Mario’s, we keep things simple and real. Our dough is made fresh daily, our sauce is crafted from ripe tomatoes and family recipes passed down through generations, and every pizza is baked to perfection in our stone ovens. Whether you’re grabbing a quick slice on your lunch break or sharing a whole pie with friends, we believe pizza should always taste like home.

Join us, and become a part of Mario's Family Pizzaria.
