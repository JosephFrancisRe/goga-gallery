Halloween at the Zoo!

Join us for a spooky adventure at Chittenden County Zoo

What to Bring

  • Comfortable shoes — you’ll be walking between animal exhibits.
  • A reusable candy bag or bucket for trick-or-treating.
  • Costumes (family-friendly, please!) — prizes for the best ones!
  • Weather-appropriate clothing — the event goes on rain or shine.
  • Your excitement for spooky fun and animal adventures!

What Not to Bring or Do

  • Do not bring outside food or candy — we provide safe treats.
  • Please avoid loud noises or flashing lights that could scare animals.
  • No costumes that fully cover faces or include realistic weapons.
  • Do not feed or tap on animal enclosures.
  • Please respect staff directions for everyone’s safety.

Halloween Activities

  • Trick-or-Treat Trail: Collect candy at themed stations around the zoo.
  • Haunted Habitat Tour: Explore spooky animal enclosures after dark!
  • Pumpkin Enrichment: Watch animals play with and eat pumpkins.
  • Costume Contest: Win prizes for the best costumes in different age groups.
  • Spooky Storytime: Gather ‘round for ghostly animal tales under the stars.
