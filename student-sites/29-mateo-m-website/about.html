About Franchise

In pop culture, anime is a popular media format especialy in younger, and more chronically online demographics. In anime, there is a fairly common character type, where a character has certain animal features like ears, and a tail. Umamusume is a multimedia franchise created by Japanese company Cygames. The main focus in this franchise are characters called Umamusume. They have the animal features of a horse, like ears and a tail. In the lore, they are said to love running, so they compete in races. They are named and themed after real life race horses, and so the characters often have silly names. Cygames stared the franchise with an anime in 2018, likely to build hype for the game they planed to realease soon. Usualy it's the other way around, game first then anime later. This didn't end up being a smart decision in the end as the game was delayed by about 3 years, and so the game release roughly coensided releasing a few months before the second season. And so the game Umamusume: Pretty Derby was released in Japan in Febuary 2021 after many delays.

Global Release

In July of 2025 Cygames released a global release with text translation into most languages including english. This brought about a massive wave of intrest from the west, Eupope, and South Asian communities who hadn't had a chance to play. The game is under the game catagory of gatcha, which is essentialy a discriptor meaning a free to play mobile game in whch luck is the primary game mechanic, this usualy involves using free accumilated currency to aquire characters and other collectables. To make money they offer microtrasactions to obtain paid currecy often used for the same aqisitions. This label is widely seen as pretty degenerate, as it ensentiveses exessive spending, and gambling as primary game mechanics, but many fall into the trap anyways. Even so, apon global release a few streamers picked it up, and eventualy it reached the stream of popular english speaking streamer NorthernLion. This got the game a bunch of attention. Since the game can be downloaded for free from the popular game store Steam, this attention got the game onto Steam's monthly most downloaded games list.
