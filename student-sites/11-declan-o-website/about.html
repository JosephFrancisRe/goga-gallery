About Us

The National City Zoo provides a safe place for all animals.


Founded in 1969, The National City Zoo is focused on educating the youth about the safety of animals and endangered species.


Started by our founder Bobby Halingtonschmurburg in 1983, the National City Zoo has been a staple of the city for decades. Our founder first started his story over 40 years ago, when he found an abandoned lion on the side of the road. He named it Emanuel and taught him many tricks. With his extreme wealth, he realized just how many animals also needed the same help that Emanuel did. And so, The National City Zoo was founded. The Zoo has dedicated all of its funding to the research of these animals and how we can improve their lives for the better. All of our animals were rescues who could not be released back into the wild.
