Animal Spotlight!


Saxton

Saxton is a star attraction of the National City Zoo. Standing at a massive 8'4 and weighing over 4,000 Pounds, Saxtons favorite hobbies are eating watermelon, sleeping, and swimming in the cool ponds with his mates on Sunday Afternoon.

Guy is a gorilla able to communicate through sign language and knows over 50 different words. Docile in nature, Guy likes to chew on bamboo and throw his excrement at the windows of his enclosure. Other hobbies include hanging from his tire swing and skipping rocks on the water.

Tipsy got his name because of how he leans when he is sleepy. Weighing only 50 pounds, this baby panda is quite rambunctious for its age. It is expected that at full growth, he will weigh over 300 pounds! That's impressive for a vegan. Hobbies include screaming for attention and climbing on his mothers back.
