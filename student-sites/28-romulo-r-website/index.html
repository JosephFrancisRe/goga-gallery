Welcome to Romulo's Pizzeria

This pizzeria was founded by Romulo 30 years ago. It is known for serving fresh, melty, crisp and savory pizza. There are many stores that serve pizza, but this is the best option for you. With very good customer service, quick food prepare and a variety of foods and drinks beyond just pizza, Romulo's Pizza will live up to the hype and satisfy you. The fan favorite of all time is their Garlic Knots, give it a try.
