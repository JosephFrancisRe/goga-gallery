Creatures of the Lovecraftian Universe

From the slumbering Great old Ones to nameless horrors lurking in forgotten seas, these creatures defy understanding and mock the sanity of those who glimpse them.

Supreme Gods (Outer Gods)

Azathoth

The blind idiot god the nuclear chaos at the center of infinity, attended by mindless flutes

Yog-Sothoth

An all-knowing being existing across dimensions; "the key and the gate."

Nyarlathotep

The crawling chaos, a shape shifting messenger who walks among mortals

Daoloth

The render of the veils a being revealing the hidden dimensions beyond perception.

Great Old Ones

Cthulhu

The high priest of the Great Old Ones, sleeping in R’lyeh until the stars align once more.

Shub-Niggurath

The Black Goat of the Woods with a Thousand Young. Source of unholy fertility.

Hastur

The unspeakable One, whose name brings madness and despair to those who utter it.

Nodens

Lord of the Great Abyss, one of the few Elder beings opposed to the horrors of chaos.

Lesser servitor Races

Shantak

Enormous,nightmarish flying beasts serving Nyarlathotep in the Dreamlands

Shoggoth

Amorhphous masses of protoplasm,created as slaves by the Elder Things.

Nightgaunt

Silent, faceless winged creatures that torment dreamers in the night

Deep Ones

Aquatic humanoids who worship Dagon and interbreed with coastal humans.
