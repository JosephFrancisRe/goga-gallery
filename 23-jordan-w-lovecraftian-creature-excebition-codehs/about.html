About the Exhibition

The lovecraftian Creature Exhibition explores the unknown horrors and cosmic entities created by H.P. Lovecraft. It hightlights their mythos, origin, and how they symbolize humanity's fear of the incomprehensible

  • The hierarchy of deities and monsters
  • Descriptions of each creature and their domains
  • Origins based on Lovecraft's mythos
  • Artistic interpretations and cultural influences
