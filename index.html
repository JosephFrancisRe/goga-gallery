About the Showcase

Gateway Tech Software Engineering

The GOGA Software Engineering Showcase highlights student work as a portfolio of creative problem-solving, design, programming, debugging, and technical communication. Projects may include games, websites, and other interactive digital artifacts developed through the Software Engineering pathway.

This gallery is designed for families, students, faculty, and community members to explore student work in a clear, accessible, and event-friendly format.
