The story on how Hachi's Pawsome Pizzeria came to be is an amazing one filled with hardship and victory

Our story starts in 1978, Hachi the main man himself arrives in the big apple. At first things were overwhelming but after he got used to his new life, he begun to think.... Where are the restruants for cats? Right then and there he made it his life's goal to make the best pizzeria for cats EVER. After tons of cat elbow grease and hardships all around him, he came up with this fine establisment. Where cats can be cats and humans can be the bystanders for once.
